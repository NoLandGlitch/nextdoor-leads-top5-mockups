The Heart of the Kitchen

Wood-Fired Since Day One

The brick oven is what sets Jo Jo's apart. Every pizza starts with hand-stretched dough and goes into a real wood-fired oven — the kind of crust you can't get from a conveyor belt. Charred edges, bubbling cheese, perfectly cooked in minutes.

It's the same approach applied to everything we make: real ingredients, real technique, served fresh. No shortcuts. That's what keeps families coming back week after week.
